# Identity

> Stories and principles related to identity.

**26 stories** explore this theme.

## Principles

- Value community and hard work as central virtues. _(P1_S01)_
- Early influences (home, school, church) are foundational to character development. _(P1_S01)_
- Remembering origins helps explain later choices and identity. _(P1_S01)_
- Hands-on community activities and local institutions provide practical learning opportunities. _(P1_S01)_
- High-quality teaching produces lasting advantage and prepares for future success. _(P1_S02)_
- Extracurricular activities build skills, confidence, and social bonds. _(P1_S02)_
- Leaders must develop delegation skills rather than assuming excessive responsibility. _(P1_S02)_
- Adversity can be an opportunity to learn durable skills. _(P1_S02)_
- High standards and disciplined practice produce lasting knowledge and character. _(P1_S03)_
- Private, firm encouragement motivates improvement more effectively than public scolding. _(P1_S03)_

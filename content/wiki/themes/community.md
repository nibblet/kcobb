# Community

> Stories and principles related to community.

**18 stories** explore this theme.

## Principles

- Value community and hard work as central virtues. _(P1_S01)_
- Early influences (home, school, church) are foundational to character development. _(P1_S01)_
- Remembering origins helps explain later choices and identity. _(P1_S01)_
- Hands-on community activities and local institutions provide practical learning opportunities. _(P1_S01)_
- High standards and disciplined practice produce lasting knowledge and character. _(P1_S03)_
- Private, firm encouragement motivates improvement more effectively than public scolding. _(P1_S03)_
- Consistent intellectual curiosity and exposure to literature expand students' horizons in limited-opportunity settings. _(P1_S03)_
- Communities can preserve and extend a leader's legacy through collective action (memorials and scholarships). _(P1_S03)_
- Lifelong engagement can come from enjoyment even without technical mastery. _(P1_S05)_
- Mentor demonstration and support can compensate for formal-skill gaps and create opportunities. _(P1_S05)_

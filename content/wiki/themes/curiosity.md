# Curiosity

> Stories and principles related to curiosity.

**23 stories** explore this theme.

## Principles

- High standards and disciplined practice produce lasting knowledge and character. _(P1_S03)_
- Private, firm encouragement motivates improvement more effectively than public scolding. _(P1_S03)_
- Consistent intellectual curiosity and exposure to literature expand students' horizons in limited-opportunity settings. _(P1_S03)_
- Communities can preserve and extend a leader's legacy through collective action (memorials and scholarships). _(P1_S03)_
- Leaders should model the behaviors they expect from others. _(P1_S04)_
- Require genuine effort before granting rewards to instill discipline and competence. _(P1_S04)_
- Consistent mentorship during formative years shapes long-term character and choices. _(P1_S04)_
- Use structured milestones and visible recognition to motivate progress and learning. _(P1_S04)_
- Embed charity and service as routine practices to reinforce citizenship. _(P1_S04)_
- Lifelong engagement can come from enjoyment even without technical mastery. _(P1_S05)_

# Integrity

> Stories and principles related to integrity.

**13 stories** explore this theme.

## Principles

- Lead by example rather than by instruction. _(P1_S06)_
- Maintain calm composure under stress; model steadiness. _(P1_S06)_
- Prioritize financial responsibility and avoid debt. _(P1_S06)_
- Respect and trust others; assume positive regard. _(P1_S06)_
- Diligent work ethic and consistent service define leadership. _(P1_S06)_
- Lead through industriousness and skill mastery to create economic opportunity. _(P1_S07)_
- When constrained, pursue practical compromises rather than ideal solutions to make progress. _(P1_S07)_
- Teach practical skills through hands-on instruction and close supervision to build competence. _(P1_S07)_
- Maintain very high standards and enforce them consistently to instill discipline. _(P1_S07)_
- Instill work as a fundamental part of childhood development to build habits and responsibility. _(P1_S09)_

## About Steve Swink

Steve Swink was a personal friend of Keith's since childhood and a fellow Eagle Scout from Troop 40 in Calhoun City.

Steve and Keith both reached the Life rank in Boy Scouts and remained stuck there for a couple of years, with "other things on our minds," before eventually buckling down to complete the rigorous twenty-one merit badge program required for Eagle Scout (P1_S04). Decades later, Steve became CEO of Dispatch Management Services Corp., a public delivery company in New York with logistics technology. At Steve's request, Keith joined the board, and also persuaded two friends—Ned Allen and Tom Saporito—to join. The business was troubled from the start, Steve's attempted turnaround was unsuccessful, and Steve resigned, followed by the other directors (P1_S26). Keith characterized this board experience as "a fairly miserable experience for us all" (P1_S26).

**Notable moments**

- Both Steve and Keith became Eagle Scouts under Dr. Carter Dobbs' direction, part of an unusually large group of eleven boys from Troop 40 to attain the rank (P1_S04)
- Invited Keith to join the board of Dispatch Management Services Corp. in New York, which ended unsuccessfully when Steve resigned from his CEO role (P1_S26)

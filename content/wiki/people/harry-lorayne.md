## About Harry Lorayne

Harry Lorayne was a memory-training specialist whose book *How to Develop a Super Power Memory* Keith discovered and mastered during his recovery from lung surgeries in high school.

While sick and recovering from the first of two collapsed lung surgeries, Keith ordered Lorayne's newly published book and spent hours practicing the memory improvement techniques. Though relatively simple, the tricks worked amazingly well. Keith mastered skills that served him for years: memorizing abstract lists, associating faces with names, and remembering hundreds of people. Later in college, he estimated he could call over three thousand students by name. Keith and his friend John Simpson would win bar bets by having Keith memorize fifty random words after hearing them once—winnings that paid for many nights out on the town. Lorayne became famous over the following years as the foremost memory-training specialist in the world, a status Keith found entirely understandable. The techniques saved Keith untold hours of studying throughout college, and the skills contributed significantly to his academic momentum at the University of Southern Mississippi (P1_S02, P1_S13).

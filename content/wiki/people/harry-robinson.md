## About Harry Robinson

Harry Robinson was a CPA with the M.M. Winkler & Associates accounting firm in Bruce, Mississippi, who employed Keith during the summer between his sophomore and junior years of college.

Keith worked for Harry Robinson in the summer of 1961, gaining professional experience in public accounting that built upon his earlier work at Lunceford's Bargain Store and other ventures (P1_S09). This position represented an important step in Keith's career development, coming after his high school years of entrepreneurship and retail management and before his prestigious internship with Humble Oil & Refining Co. the following summer. The job also coincided with a pivotal summer in Keith's personal life—it was during this period of working for Robinson that Keith began spending evenings at the Hill family home down the street, where his relationship with Dot deepened from casual neighborhood friendship into something more serious (P1_S12). The accounting work with Robinson provided Keith with early exposure to professional practice in his chosen field, complementing the academic training he was receiving at the University of Southern Mississippi, where he was majoring in accounting.

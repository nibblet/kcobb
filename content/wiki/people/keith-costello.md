## About Keith Costello

Keith Costello is the CEO of Locality Bank, a bank Keith Cobb helped organize as an original investor approximately three and a half years before the interview. Keith describes Costello as "an excellent leader and an excellent banker," crediting him as key to the bank's success.

Costello serves as chairman of the Florida Bankers Association, a significant role Keith notes alongside his bank leadership (IV_S08). What Keith particularly admires is Costello's communication practice: he writes regular messages—either weekly or bi-weekly—to all employees detailing his activities over that period. Keith describes the breadth of activity in these messages as "staggering" and "unbelievable," and views them as leadership by example, showing employees his level of community involvement and professional engagement. Keith had breakfast with Costello to discuss these practices, demonstrating an ongoing mentoring relationship between the two men (IV_S08).

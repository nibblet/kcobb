## About Paul Breazeale

Paul Breazeale was a contemporary and colleague of Keith's at Peat Marwick in Jackson, Mississippi, and later joined him as one of the founding staff members of the Orlando office.

Keith first knew Paul as one of several contemporaries in the Jackson office whom he respected during his early years there (P1_S18). When Keith was building the Orlando office from scratch in 1971, he persuaded Paul to transfer from Jackson to Florida—described as "a huge leap of faith"—and Paul became especially helpful during those formative years, working hard to help establish the new office (P1_S20). After a few years in Orlando, Paul moved back to Jackson and established his own accounting firm, which grew into what Keith describes as "a flagship business in Jackson," with Paul becoming "a distinguished professional in the community" (P1_S20).

The Breazeale family remained dear and valuable friends to Keith, who notes that he stays in touch with Paul regularly (P1_S20).

**Notable moments**

- Listed among the contemporaries Keith respected during his Jackson years, alongside Murray Underwood, Atlee Harmon, Mike Green, and others (P1_S18)
- Transferred from Jackson to Orlando as part of Keith's skeleton staff for the new office, one of four experienced personnel recruited from other offices (P1_S20)
- Provided especially valuable help during the challenging early years of building the Orlando office before returning to Jackson to establish his own firm (P1_S20)

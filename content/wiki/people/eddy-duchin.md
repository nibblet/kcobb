## About Eddy Duchin

Eddy Duchin was a pianist whose life story, told in a 1950s film, introduced Keith to piano music that would become central to his lifelong love of classical and jazz compositions.

*The Eddy Duchin Story*, filmed in the mid-1950s and starring Tyrone Power and Kim Novak, featured piano music performed by Carmen Cavalaro. Keith saw the movie and "absolutely adored the music, particularly the theme song, 'To Love Again,'" which was based on Chopin's "Nocturne in E Flat." This single song remained with Keith for more than fifty years. He acquired the original soundtrack and played it regularly on his iPhone and home Sonos system, describing how he would play it "over and over again." The "Nocturne" became what Keith called his "personal theme song," effectively defining his love for piano music (P1_S31).

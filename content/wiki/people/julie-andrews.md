## About Julie Andrews

Julie Andrews was the original star of the Broadway musical *My Fair Lady*, which Keith saw as a spellbinding seventeen-year-old in New York City.

Keith saw Andrews perform alongside Rex Harrison in the original cast of *My Fair Lady* on Broadway during a trip to New York City with the Lion's All-State Band (P1_S11). The experience left a lasting impression—he listened to the soundtrack of that performance hundreds of times over the years, and each time he heard the overture theme and "I Could Have Danced All Night," the same goosebumps appeared on his arms that had first risen when he sat mesmerized in the orchestra seats of the Broadway Theater. Keith described it as "truly a spellbinding experience" and asked rhetorically, "How could one ever forget such an experience?" (P1_S11). Decades later, Andrews remained a touchstone for Keith's relationship with music. In reflecting on his favorite musical compositions, he quoted her performance in *The Sound of Music*: "I simply remember my favorite things, and then I don't feel so bad" (P1_S31).

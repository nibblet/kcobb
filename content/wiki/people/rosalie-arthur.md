## About Rosalie Arthur

Rosalie Arthur was an exceptional accountant who worked under Keith at KPMG's Fort Lauderdale office and later became Mike Egan's chief of staff at Alamo Rent A Car.

Keith recruited Rosalie as part of his deliberate effort to build a "superb cadre" of top accounting talent in Fort Lauderdale during the 1980s. He considered her one of the "cornerstones" of his success there and remembered her among the "outstanding people" whose pristine quality was recognized throughout the business community (P1_S21). She was later recruited away by Mike Egan at Alamo—along with Mary Wood and Jill Vales—where she rose to the position of chief of staff. In that role, she handled sensitive operational matters for Egan and served as Keith's trusted liaison when he negotiated his CEO contract in 1995. Keith specifically sought her out to print his detailed term sheet before meeting with Mike, indicating the confidence both men placed in her (P1_S25). Keith tracked her career evolution over twenty-five years with evident pride, noting that many from his Fort Lauderdale staff became senior financial executives and "superstars in a wide array of businesses" (P1_S21).

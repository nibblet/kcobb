## About John Edmondson

John Edmondson was one of Keith's best friends during high school in Calhoun City, someone with whom he shared many common interests and spent a lot of time (P1_S02).

John was a drummer and a member of the Dixieland jazz band that Keith and several other high school musicians formed in tenth grade. The group included Hal Jr. Reese on trumpet, Phil Ray on trombone, Sonny Young on bass, and Keith on clarinet (P1_S05). Despite their limited formal training, the band was "actually pretty good" and performed at various events and talent shows, eventually competing in the Mid-South Fair Talent Competition in Memphis. Though they didn't win the competition, they were featured on a local Memphis TV show and had the opportunity to meet Elvis Presley (P1_S05).

**Notable moments**

- Played drums in the Dixieland jazz band with Keith during high school, performing at events and talent shows (P1_S05)

## About Steve Berrard

Steve Berrard was Wayne Huizenga's right-hand man at AutoNation, who reached out to Keith the day after Keith had floated the idea of AutoNation acquiring Alamo in a parking lot conversation with Wayne.

Despite Wayne Huizenga's initial brush-off when Keith approached him about AutoNation acquiring Alamo at the Performing Arts Center parking lot, Steve called Keith the very next day asking to meet and discuss the car rental business, revealing that AutoNation did in fact have interest (P1_S25). This contact helped keep AutoNation "on the back burner" during Keith's parallel negotiations with Henry Silverman at Cendant. When those Cendant talks collapsed due to Mike Egan's abrupt rejection, Mike called Wayne Huizenga the next day, and negotiations with AutoNation—facilitated by Steve's earlier outreach—moved quickly. Six weeks later, the deal closed with Wayne acquiring Alamo for $625 million, rounding up from Cendant's $600 million offer (P1_S25).

## Full Text

_Transcript edited lightly for clarity and readability; wording and voice preserved. Choosing KPMG and the career path continue in **The KPMG Journey (Interview)**._

**Keith:** I'm from Mississippi. I grew up in a small town in North Mississippi—a little town of about two thousand people. I learned a lot in that small world, a microcosm of society. We had a wonderful education there—unique for a community that size. It's in a part of Mississippi that has red clay hills—hence the title *Out of the Red Clay Hills*. I left after high school for college, but I've maintained roots there—I'm still tethered to it.

**Cagnetta:** You went to college in Mississippi.

**Keith:** I did—the University of Southern Mississippi.

**Cagnetta:** Right—and you graduated. How did you wind up at KPMG?

## What This Story Shows

- A small community can provide an education and value system as strong as any large one.
- Your origins stay with you — they are a foundation, not a limitation.
